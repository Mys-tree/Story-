And she was terrified. Terrified of every decision or choice she made, thinking all the time that it would lead to more downs than ups
That was the consequence, the consequence of lack of acknowledgement and lack of love.
Who she had been as a child, how she had been as a child - silenced, unseen, unheard, never ever enough.
A mother who was scared, terrified that anyone coming near her children would take their love away.
She did not feel or see the love, the unquestionning love of her children.
